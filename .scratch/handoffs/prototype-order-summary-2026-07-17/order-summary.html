Order summary — layout cleanup

The materials estimate currently forces every material category and the cost totals into one rigid 4-column grid, so on tablets the columns cram and the big Total lands wherever the grid drops it. The proposal keeps the same content but lets the material categories reflow to fit any width ("small print", auto-fill), and lifts the cost summary into its own panel that becomes a side rail when there's room and stacks underneath when there isn't.
