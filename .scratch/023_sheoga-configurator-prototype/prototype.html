nedSheoga configurator — prototypes

Sheoga sells by description, not SKU — species × grade × width × solid/engineered, plus texture, lengths, edges and finishing, and a separate wood-vent & damper program. Both prototypes below run a real pricing engine built from the three sheets (Feb 2025 flooring list, Feb 2022 vent list, damper cost sheet). Every control reprices live; Save pins an option for side-by-side comparison while a customer goes back and forth; Recents tracks what you've priced. “Add to product line” shows exactly what would land on the job.

Why two shapes

  • A (board) is for the desk: a salesperson flipping a customer between 5¼" and 6¼", Clear and Character, White Oak and Hickory wants every lever visible with prices on the chips — width chips each carry their live price, so “show me it wider” is one click, and the full species × width grid is one more.
  • B (guided) is for the phone at the counter or on-site: one decision per screen, big targets, running price pinned to the bottom bar, review screen at the end that saves into the same trays.

    • What both share

  • The same data module and pricing engine — one source of truth fed from the three sheets.
  • The description is the SKU: every configuration renders a canonical order description (what you read to Sheoga on the phone) and that string is what gets snapshotted onto the job line.
  • Saved options + recents persist in localStorage here; in the app they'd live on the customer record (saved) and per-user (recents).

    • Pricing-engine assumptions (to confirm with Sheoga / the sheets)

  • Length upcharges (+5% … +30%) are applied to the unfinished base price incl. no-sap adders, before flat $/sf adders for texture, edge and finishing. The sheet just says “Add 15%” — base is our reading.
  • Prefinished small-order fees (<500 sf +$300, <250 sf +$600) apply whenever a finish is selected; shown amortized into the $/sf at the entered job size.
  • Established-stain adder picks $1.95 or $2.85 automatically from the selected texture (Sawcut / Bandsawn / Aged Brush ⇒ $2.85), per the custom-finishing sheet.
  • “Cubed” vents price as the matching vent size + $10.00; damper add-on = stocking damper price + $5.00 attach; flush-vent frames at $0.40 × (L + 2W) lineal inches.
  • Custom color match ($750 flat) and non-stock prefinished colors are flagged as “call Sheoga” rather than priced.
