✋ Retiring the grab icon — two ideas you can try right here

Today every row carries a little hand icon just for dragging. Both ideas below remove it: the row gets cleaner, and moving a row becomes something you do to the row itself.

A · Hold the type chip to drag
The colored H/T/M chip is already the row's "handle" visually. Hover it — it hints with a dashed ring. Press and hold ~a third of a second and the row lifts, ready to drag. Try it below (mouse or touch).

B · Right-click the row for a move menu
Right-click is completely unused in the app today. Right-click any row below — moving stops being a physical drag and becomes a menu choice, which also gives us a free home for Duplicate and Delete.

Recommendation: ship both, drop the hand icon. The chip-hold keeps the fast physical drag the app already has (the 220ms hold-to-arm logic exists — it would just move from the hand icon to the chip), and the right-click menu is the discoverable, no-dexterity fallback that also works for "move to another area" without scrolling. On touch, long-pressing the chip opens the same menu instead of dragging. First time someone hovers a chip, a one-time tooltip ("hold to move · right-click for options") teaches it.
