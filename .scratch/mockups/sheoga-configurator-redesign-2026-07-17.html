Layout changes in this version

  • 2×2 grid order is Texture · Finishing / Lengths · Edge.
  • Construction left, Grade beside it, and the price-grid button right-aligned on that same row (Unfinished).
  • Stocked tab: Grade centered with the grid button beside it.
  • Order line joins with plain spaces — no dots.

Sheen & stain rules

  • Prefinished finishes reveal a Sheen dropdown (30 / 20 / 15 / 10 / 5 + Custom…). Established & custom colors also get a Stain color dropdown + Custom….
  • Sheen on the Unfinished tab is always free — it's a made-to-order spec.
  • On the Stocked tab only, moving a product off its standard sheen adds a $250 flat line. Standards: 30 default; 20 for Nutmeg, Caramel, Cattail·Sawcut, Vintage-Charm Hickory Nut; 5 for Fresh Cut & Frost.
