Price books — one library (C, refined)

The sidebar book-list is gone: "Price book" in Settings opens this board. Click any book to go inside — a portal-fed book carries its source sheet strip at the top; in-house books get a plain import strip. New in this round — review at your own pace: hitting refresh only downloads. The row shows a little progress bar, flashes a ✓, then the control becomes a moss Review pill with the change count — click it whenever you're ready. When several books are waiting, a floating Review all bar walks you through them one after another. Try it: hit the column download on Virginia Tile, or check a few boxes and "Refresh selected".

What replaced what: the old sidebar's Stock / Special-order lists → the board columns · the "Vendor sheets" tab → gone (its board absorbed the books) · the sheet row's refresh → the row/strip refresh · "Feeds <book>" ⋯ item → unnecessary (the sheet lives in the book) · drop-zone → the strip under the top bar · bookmarklet setup + paste → the "Paste sign-in" popover. Batch refresh: check rows across columns, the bar appears. Download and review are separate steps: refresh (row, column, or batch) only fetches; each book then waits with a Review pill, and the floating bar's "Review all" chains the pending reviews back-to-back. "Later" keeps a book waiting — nothing is applied until you say so.
